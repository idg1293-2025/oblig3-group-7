The World is a Mess — Let's Fix It!

As part of IDG1293 Advanced CSS we have created a story focusing on the UNs SDG goal 14. Now we are going to tell you this story throught the screen.

Welcome to a journey that plunges you deep into the heart of our oceans, where the fate of marine life is hanging by a thread. SDG Goal 14, "Life Below Water," is our call to action to protect the seas from pollution, overfishing, and climate change. But here’s the twist – we’re not just talking about the crisis. We’re about to take you on an interactive adventure! Through the magic of scroll-telling, dynamic design, and animated storytelling, we’re bringing the message of SDG 14 to life in a way that grabs attention, sparks action, and hopefully sparks the interest. This isn’t just about writing code — it’s about making waves, raising awareness, and showing the world how powerful we can be when we join forces to protect our oceans. Ready to dive in? Let’s make some noise for the oceans!
